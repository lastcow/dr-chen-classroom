1.1 What Is Information Security?

Information security (often abbreviated infosec ) is the practice of protecting information and information systems from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction.

The goal is to ensure that data remains private, accurate, and accessible to those who legitimately need it.From the Week 01 reading material
