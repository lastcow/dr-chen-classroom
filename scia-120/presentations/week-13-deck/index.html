13.4.5 Shadow IT

Core explanation

Classroom interpretation

When individual developers spin up EC2 instances, S3 buckets, or SaaS subscriptions outside formal procurement processes, those resources may bypass security controls, not be inventoried, and not be monitored.

13.8.1 AWS CloudTrail and CloudWatch

Core explanation

AWS CloudTrail records all API calls made in an AWS account — who made the call, from where, at what time, and what was the result.

Classroom interpretation
